Who We Want To Coach


Our program is a fit for your if:
You are interested in growing your business and adding more employees.
Your business is located in a community where annual family income is $35,000 or less.
You are committed to keeping your business in a low-income area.
You have five or fewer employees.
Your business earns less than $40,000 in annual revenue.
